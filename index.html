About GreenCare

With GreenCare, imagine a world where nature and community thrive together. We are a non-profit organization dedicated to protecting the environment and empowering people. We work everywhere from saving forests to cleaning waterways and planting trees in cities. Our mission is to spread awareness and create a greener, healthier future for all.

Our Projects

Forest Restoration

Our goal is to restore and protect forests worldwide. Let's work together to save the lungs of the planet.

Waterway Cleanup

Clean rivers and waterways are vital for our ecosystem. Participate in our cleanup drives and fight against pollution.

Urban Tree Planting

Make cities greener and help reduce urban heat. Let's make a difference by planting trees in our communities.

Beach Cleanup

Join us in freeing our beaches from plastic. Clean oceans are essential for a healthy planet.
